WAITING ROOM

You are currently waiting for two other Turkers to arrive, so that you can begin. In the mean time, here is some more information about the task. In this task you will be working together to solve a challenging puzzle.

When the task begins you will be given the puzzle (everyone in your group is working on the same puzzle) and some clues to help solve the puzzle. You will also be provided with a shared spreadsheet to mark down your guesses and answers (everyone in your group will be able to see and edit the same spreadsheet).

You will also be able to communicate with other Turkers in your group. Depending on your condition you will either be able to communicate using a group chat or an online forum. You will be able to type and submit messages to your group. They will see your messages (and you will see theirs) soon after they are submitted. We encourage you to use the communication channel to work together to solve this challenging problem.

Groups that successfully solve the problem will be given a bonus.
